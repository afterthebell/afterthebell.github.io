    About The Makers
    Our game took inspiration from some visual novel games, such as Doki Doki Literature Club, and some point-and-click games, such as Elevator Hitch. Even if you don't see it, bullying is still a prevalent issue, especially online, and we hoped to inform people about the dangers of bullying through an immersive story that could stick with you.
